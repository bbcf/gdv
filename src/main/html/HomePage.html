GDV stands for Genome Data Viewer. Itâ€™s goal is to provide biologists and bio-analysts a fast, browsable and editable view of their data along the genome.

The user will be able to launch different statistics modules on his data and thus prepare all the mining process of the analysis. GDV is a web application, and will never be downloaded, but will be accessible at any time, directly from a web-browser. So, an user can access his data everywhere, and also share it with other people which make it interesting if the user collaborate with other entities that are not in the same site or even in the same countries.
